What is our goal?

What is a good story? Is it simply creative jargon splattered on a page made to make oneself think of far off places? Or is it a device that can take readers to new worlds, meet new friends and do new things they've never dreamt of? Here at Conduire, we strive to share experiences that don't just make you think of extraordinary tales; but to believe them.
